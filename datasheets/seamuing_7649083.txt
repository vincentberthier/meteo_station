This 3.7V 10000mAh LiPo lithium battery has a PH2.0A connector and can be used for Raspberry Pi UPS board whose socket is PH2.0A and other project devices with PH2.0A connector.

Specifications:
Standard Voltage: 3.7V
Capacity: 10000mAh
Maximum load: Operating current: 3A
Dimensions (± 2 mm): 115 x 65 x 8.8 mm (L x W x H).
Over current protection: about 1.8-2.1A
Plug type: PH2.0A.
Weight: 155g

Standard charging method: charge this battery at 4.25V at 0.2C constant current, then charge the battery at a constant voltage of 4.2V until the charging current is ≤0.05C
